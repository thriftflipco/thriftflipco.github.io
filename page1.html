About Us

Thrift-Flip Co. aims to promote and support sustainable and budget-friendly shopping especially for students and teenagers. Thrift stores offers clothing items in nearly-perfect condition and gives the feeling of satisfaction of unearthing a gorgeous piece of clothing for half its original price. It shows the most famous thrift stores around the City of Malolos and the different comments and feedback of the customers towards these stores. 

Mara Adriano is a 17 year old student from Bulacan State University-Laboratory High School who loves fashion, beauty and lifestyle. Through her enthusiasm on these field, she created her own Youtube channel where she uploads her weekly vlogs doing what she loves most, shopping and makeup.
